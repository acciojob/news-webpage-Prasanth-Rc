The FCA has expanded its probe into workplace harassment and bullying within the financial sector. Engaging with multiple banks and insurers, the regulator seeks to address non-financial misconduct following a rise in reported cases. While no enforcement actions have been initiated, the FCA emphasizes its commitment to upholding firm culture and handling misconduct appropriately.
